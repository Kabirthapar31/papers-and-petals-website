Resin Art Care

Handle your resin pieces gently and clean them with a soft cloth.

  • Use a slightly damp microfiber cloth to wipe resin surfaces. Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaning pads.
  • Keep resin art away from direct sunlight for extended periods to prevent yellowing or fading.
  • Avoid placing very hot items directly on resin coasters or trays.

Jewelry Care

Our earrings and jewellery pieces are handmade and delicate; treat them with care.

  • Store jewellery in a dry place and avoid contact with water, perfumes, or lotions.
  • Clean metal parts gently with a soft cloth. Do not use abrasive cleaners on resin or gem components.
  • Handle ear hooks and findings carefully to avoid bending.
