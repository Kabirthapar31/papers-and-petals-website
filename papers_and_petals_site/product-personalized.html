Description

Create a candle as unique as your loved one. Start by choosing your favourite scent and jar colour, then enter a name or short message to be engraved on the vessel. Our artisans carefully engrave your text, ensuring a beautiful and lasting result. The perfect gift for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, or any special moment.

Care & Safety

Allow the wax to melt completely across the top on the first burn. Trim the wick before each burn and never leave your candle unattended. Follow our general candle care guidelines for best results.
